Make Umuzi Better(MUB)

We were briefed to Make Umuzi Better by using the process of Design Thinking. After thorough analysis of our insights from our user sample, we opted to focus on the response time during the early stage of the recruitment process. The objective of the project was to Make Umuzi Better as the name suggests. The task at hand
was to make response time to applicants faster and seamless.
Through deliberations we came up with a solution which was to send an
sms after submission of an application to an Umuzi applicant acknoledging receipt of that application.
